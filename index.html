Chicken Tikka Masala

click here
Ingredients
  • 1/2 lb chicken
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Spices (garam masala, paprika, turmeric)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Heavy cream
Instructions
  • In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, 1 tablespoon of oil, garlic, ginger, garam masala, paprika, turmeric, cumin, coriander, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add chicken pieces to the marinade, coat them thoroughly, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (overnight is best).
  • Heat the remaining oil in a large skillet. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  • Stir in tomato sauce and heavy cream. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the sauce thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with rice or naan

Vegetable Biryani

click here
Ingredients
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Assorted veggies (e.g., peas, carrots, bell peppers)
  • Biryani spices (garam masala, cumin seeds)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions
  • Heat oil in a large pot. Add bay leaves, cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and cumin seeds. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for another minute.
  • Add mixed vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add 2 cups of water, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  • Garnish with cilantro and fried onions before serving.

Palak Paneer

Ingredients
  • 1 cup paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 cups spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Spices (garam masala, cumin seeds, red chili powder, turmeric)
Instructions
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and sauté until they splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent.
  • Add garlic and ginger and sauté for another minute.
  • Add the chopped spinach and cook until it wilts down.
  • Allow the mixture to cool, then blend it into a smooth paste.
  • Return the paste to the pan, add cream, and simmer for a few minutes
  • Add paneer cubes and cook until heated through.
  • Serve with naan or rice.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients
  • 1 cup paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Spices (garam masala, paprika, cumin)
  • Butter or oil for cooking
Instructions
  • Heat oil in a pan and add the chopped onions. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute.
  • Stir in the garam masala, paprika, turmeric, and cayenne pepper.
  • Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil begins to separate from the mixture.
  • Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  • Reduce heat and add the heavy cream and yogurt. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with naan or rice.

Chana Masala

Ingredients
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions
  • Heat oil in a pan and add the chopped onions. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  • Stir in garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt.
  • Add the drained chickpeas and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.

Aloo Gobi

Ingredients
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cubed

  • 1 onion, finely chopped

  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions
  • Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Sauté until they sizzle.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they become golden brown.
  • Add garlic and ginger, and cook dd the tomato for another minute.
  • Stir in turmeric, coriander, cayenne pepper, and salt.
  • Add the cauliflower florets and potatoes. Cook for 10-15 minutes until they are tender.
